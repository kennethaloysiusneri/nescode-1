Chelsea Champions of London

Chelsea F.C... Chelsea Football Club is a professional football club based in London, that competes in the Premier League, of which they are reigning champions. Founded in 1905, the club's home ground since then has been Stamford Bridge.

Hospitality

Experience a taste of hospitality at Chelsea FC. Our hospitality offering at Chelsea FC is unrivalled and with packages to suit all styles and budgets. Our award-winning annual packages include pre and post-match dining, alongside premium seating offering stunning views of the pitch.

Fans

The Fans' Forum is a body consisting of elected fans and senior members of the club. It meets three times a season to discuss and debate club matters. The meetings take place at the stadium on home match days, usually prior to kick-off.
